Gumball and Darwin

Gumball Watterson

Darwin Watterson

Gumball often gets himself into trouble, his best friend and adopted brother Darwin usually follows Gumball into trouble but sometimes he is the only one pulling Gumball back to reality.

Darwin used to be the family pet before he grew legs and was adopted by the Wattersons. Gumball and Darwin are now inseperable!
